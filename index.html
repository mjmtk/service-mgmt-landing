About Manaaki Tech

Based in New Zealand, Manaaki Tech is committed to creating technology solutions that honor and support diverse communities worldwide. Our name "Manaaki" reflects our core values of hospitality, care, and respect for all people.

Manaaki Care represents our first step in revolutionizing how social services connect with and support families globally, ensuring cultural values are embedded in every interaction.
